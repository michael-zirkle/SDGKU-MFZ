Main Characters

The 3 main characters used throughout the game are Donald, Goofy, and Sora. Sora is a keyblade master meant to lock the doors between worlds

Many other characters appear in the game. Although every one can be traced back to the Disney or Final Fantasy franchises seen in either movies or previous videogames.
